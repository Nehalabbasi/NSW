FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions


  • Will my logo be unique?

    Yes absolutely. It will be designed exclusively for your organization, product or service. We do not use “clip art” or recycled designs.!

  • How much does a logo cost?

    It all depends on how you want your logo to be. We offer different budget friendly packages starting from $24 which is the basic package mainly for the new ventures. Our startup package starts from $69 and has some Smart design Solution Recommended For Small Scale Businesses. We also offer a complete package known as the ‘’The All-in-one’’ package for $899 which serves as a cutting-edge design solution and is recommended for all businesses.

  • Are there hidden, or additional fees for revising logos during my project?

    No. All our packages have built-in revision cycle also, our designers are flexible enough to accommodate a few more revisions without any extra charges. We do not promise you to offer unlimited revisions as we do not believe in following an unrealistic approach.

  • How many formats you will provide?

    We will provide the final logo in different formats such as:

    • EPS (editable vector source file) (CMYK)
    • GIF
    • PSD (RGB)
    • AI (Adobe Illustrator)
    • TIFF (CMYK)
    • JPG (in multiple sizes, RGB)
    • PDF
  • What is the procedure?

    It all starts with our logo design briefing form which you can complete online or download as a Word file to email back to us. You will have an account manager and a lead designer who will discuss your project with you over the telephone. If you have asked us to look at other companies’ brands, we will research these on the web. We will then prepare an initial selection of prototype designs. These are intended to start a dialogue that will continue through a series of development stages to complete the design.

  • How do I keep check of progress?

    Telephone and email have served us very well over the years and we have built great working relationships with hundreds of clients we have never met. However, you keep a complete check on your order progress right from your Customer Panel (dashboard).

  • How do revisions work?

    You will be given multiple design concepts by our designers. You will select a specific design for your logo and then we will make revisions according to your requirements. We will also ask you to give feedback on the designs already provided.

  • Do you design stationery?

    Yes, stationery design can be provided at an additional fee, if it’s not included in your package selected. We give you artwork files to take it to your printer for letterheads, business cards, compliments slip and other specialized stationery you may require.
